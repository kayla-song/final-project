Meet the Evergreens, Washington University's premiere treble a cappella group!

About

The Washington University Evergreens, formerly known as the Greenleafs, are WashU's second-oldest and premiere treble a cappella group. Founded in 1988, the Evergreens continue to cover a variety of genres with a special emphasis on female artists.

Every November, the Evergreens put on their annual concert, Green Eggs and Jam, featuring other WashU performance groups. Every April, the Evergreens perform a joint concert with the Washington Universiy Pikers, celebrating their seniors just before graduation.
